Home Mattress Cleaning

Most of us spend about a third of our lives in bed, not realizing that we’re not alone there. Almost every bed in Africa is crowded with dust mites- microscopic creatures that feed on our dead skin and thrive in dark, damp areas like carpets and mattresses. They can’t be removed with a domestic vacuum cleaner, and their excrement can cause or exacerbate allergies, asthma, eczema, hay fever and bronchitis.

Why mattress cleaning?

Because mattresses can’t be washed! Mites and microbes find ideal life and growth conditions in mattresses. Dander, dust, sweat, etc. feeds the mites with nutrients. Even the densest sheet cannot protect before those particles. Once every 6 months your bed needs to be cleaned and sanitize to kill these unwelcome beasts and their eggs.
