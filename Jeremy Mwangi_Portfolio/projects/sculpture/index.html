WELCOME TO OUR SITE

THE Sculpture WEBSITE is where you will find fabulous sculptures in a wide variety of mediums including Brass Sculptures, Bronze Sculptures, Ceramic Sculptures, Galvanised Steel, Iron Sculptures, Lead Sculptures, Metal Sculptures, Plaster Sculptures, Reconstituted Stone, Recycled Metal Sculptures, Bronze Resin Sculptures, Steel Sculptures, Stainless Steel Sculptures, Stone Sculptures, Wood Carving and Wood Sculptures, Wire Sculptures and under water sculptures. But the web is what we do but we also know that there is no substitute for seeing sculpture in fabulous settings.

We also look to promote sculpture and happily these are increasing with fabulous venues across the country to see sculpture. The range of subjects is almost endless from Abstract Garden Sculptures, Fountains and Water Features, Garden Ornaments, Planters, Garden Pots, Garden Statuary, Sculptural Metal Artwork, Garden Sundials, Wildlife Sculptures, Domestic Animal Sculptures, and Figurative Sculptures.

NELSON MANDELA

Marking the 50-year anniversary of what began Nelson Mandela's "long walk to freedom" - and the piece of land that, quite randomly, irrevocably altered the history of South Africa - is a quietly powerful new sculpture, set into the environment of this silently potent space. The sculpture, the centrepiece of a new memorial site - the Nelson Mandela Capture Site - was unveiled by South African President Jacob Zuma during the site's official inauguration on 5 August 2012.

PAIGE BRADLEY

Paige Bradley is an American sculptor known for representative figurative bronzes. Bradley became popularly known for her sculpture concept, Expansion, a work of bronze and electricity depicting a woman's figure in a cross-legged position with light emanating from cracks in her body, originally photographed in 2004 against a Manhattan skyline
