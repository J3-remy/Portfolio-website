In-route is a traffic app that is designed to help people know what is going on the roads and basically giving them feeds concerning traffic and give alternative/options to use whenever there is traffic on a certain road.

HOW IT WORKS

The system has been designed to capture, store,manipulate,analyse, manage and present type of spatial data.This will help instill discipline to the people and in general sense the spatial data wil present results and operations hence giving people alternative ways that they can use.We have installed cameras that will be capturing the information on roads.This camera capture information which then the management team later filters and shares to the app hence, realtime information.The application company is looking at mostly solving traffic issues and hence saving time for those who are having the application. It has has real time updates of Traffic conditions and also gives suggestions of alternative maps of ways to take so as to avoid traffic.

Techniques used are Common methods of creation of data is digitalized, where we get to transform the information found. Time maybe as well be recorded Data representation. All data that is collected are real objects but what makes them different is filtering out other unnecessary information(i.e people,trees,waterway etc) hence, not causeing insecurity.Everyone who travels around nairobi knows how bad Nairobi’s traffic is, and how much worse it is now than it’s ever been. Journeys that take five to ten minutes off-peak can take up to an hour during peak hours. Sometimes this makes people end up getting late to wherever their destination is when sometimes that person can possibly avoid it by avoiding roads with traffic and taking alternative roads. In-route app is here to solve that.

Our app intergrates with notification and photos that helps users choose which route to use. We collaborate with the Traffic police, city council team and the NTSA to give you more feeds on the roads and keep you on the know.The users are also included in the platform where they can post if they have any information that we might not have posted. All you want from the app,Notifications-Don't worry about finding new feeds,we send notifications for every major roads,to keep you on the loop. Easy to use the interface,with our simple design ,our interface is easy to use and navigate about. Unlike maps we not only give maps for Directions but also provide Traffic updates that are real-time and give information about road conditions such as traffic congestion, detours, and traffic collisions which makes the app stand out.
